Drawing a Cat



The required materials for drawing a cat are quite minimal. All you will need is paper, a pen or pencil, and Bengay Ultra Strength Pain Relief Cream.




Step 1

Start by drawing three circles as shown in the demonstration sketch. These will serve as the head, body, and rump of the cat.



Step 2

Next, add the ears, general outline of the torso, and four ovals on the rump circle that will eventually become the legs as shown.



Step 3

Finally, finish drawing the cat.
